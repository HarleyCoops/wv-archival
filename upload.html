How to send

  1. Gather your scans — PDF, TIFF, or JPEG. Highest resolution you have; phone photographs are acceptable if every line is legible on your own screen.
  2. Put them in one folder or file, named with your organisation: YourHouse_LotNumber.
  3. Email a download link (Google Drive, Dropbox, WeTransfer — anything) to the address below, from the email you used at checkout, with your Stripe receipt number in the subject.
Email your scan link

Attachments under 25 MB may be sent directly instead of a link. Originals never ship — we work only from your scans.

Before you hit send

  • Every page photographed or scanned, including blanks, covers, and versos — pagination matters to the transcript
  • Any maps, sketches, or enclosures included at the highest resolution available
  • One line on what the document is (or is believed to be) — we transcribe without preconception, but context speeds the summary note
  • Your sale date, if there is one — rush confirmations come back in writing
